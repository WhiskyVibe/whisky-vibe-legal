Whisky Vibe TikTok Uploader Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-16

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Whisky Vibe TikTok Uploader handles information when used to connect a TikTok account and upload short-form whisky review videos.

2. Information We Collect

  • TikTok authorization data such as access tokens, refresh tokens, granted scopes, and account identifiers
  • Basic TikTok account information returned by TikTok APIs
  • Video files, captions, hashtags, privacy settings, and related upload metadata
  • Operational logs needed to detect upload failures or API errors

3. How We Use Information

  • authenticate the connected TikTok account
  • upload authorized content to TikTok
  • refresh tokens when permitted
  • monitor upload status and resolve operational issues
  • maintain internal publishing workflows

4. Storage and Security

Sensitive credentials are intended to be stored in restricted internal environments and used only for authorized publishing workflows. Reasonable measures should be taken to protect tokens and account access.

5. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information is shared only as needed with TikTok and other platform APIs that are required to perform authorized publishing actions.

6. Retention

Authorization tokens and upload metadata may be retained only as long as necessary to operate the publishing workflow, maintain continuity, and troubleshoot failures.

7. User Control

The connected account owner may revoke TikTok authorization at any time through TikTok or by removing stored credentials from the publishing environment.

8. Third-Party Platforms

Use of this tool is also subject to TikTok's own privacy practices and platform terms.

9. Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material updates will be reflected by revising the date above.
